<p><p>The Carbon-Graphite Brush Market comprises four main types: Electrographite Brush, Graphite Brush, Metal Graphite Brush, and Silver Graphite Brush. Electrographite brushes are manufactured using high-quality graphite and are widely used in various industries due to their excellent electrical conductivity and low friction properties. Graphite brushes are made primarily of natural graphite, making them suitable for low-voltage and low-current applications. Metal graphite brushes are a combination of metal powder and graphite, offering enhanced durability and reliability. Silver graphite brushes, incorporating silver as a conductive material, are ideal for applications that require high current density and long service life.</p></p>
