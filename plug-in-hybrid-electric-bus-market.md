<p><p>A Plug-in Hybrid Electric Bus (PHEB) is a type of electric bus that combines the benefits of both electric and traditional combustion engine technology. These buses typically have an electric motor and battery, which can be charged by plugging into an external power source. They also have a small combustion engine that can be used as a backup, allowing for extended range and flexibility.</p><p>The demand for PHEBs has been steadily increasing due to various factors such as rising concerns over air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions, the need for reduced dependency on fossil fuels, and government initiatives to promote cleaner transportation options. PHEBs offer several advantages over conventional diesel buses, including lower operating costs, reduced emissions, quieter operation, and improved fuel efficiency.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the PHEB market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.4% during the forecast period.
