<p><p>The handheld military rangefinders market consists of two main types, namely the binocular rangefinder and the monocular rangefinder. Binocular rangefinders are equipped with two eyepieces, allowing for enhanced depth perception and better target identification. They are generally larger and more powerful, suitable for long-range applications. On the other hand, monocular rangefinders have a single eyepiece and are more compact and lightweight. They are ideal for quick and close-range target acquisition. Both types play a crucial role in military operations by providing accurate distance measurements to targets, assisting in aiming and artillery calculations.</p></p>
