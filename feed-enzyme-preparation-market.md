Phytase aids in the breakdown of phytic acid, enabling better utilization of phosphorus and other essential minerals. Protease assists in protein degradation, enhancing amino acid absorption and promoting growth. Carbohydrase helps break down complex carbohydrates, facilitating their conversion into energy. These enzyme types are crucial in optimizing the nutritional value of feed, resulting in improved animal health, performance, and overall productivity.</p></p>
