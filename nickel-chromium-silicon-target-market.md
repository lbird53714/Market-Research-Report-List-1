<p><p>The nickel chromium silicon target market can be categorized into two types: plane target and rotating target. Plane targets are fixed, flat surfaces used in sputtering techniques for depositing thin films onto substrates. They are suitable for large-scale production and uniform film deposition. On the other hand, rotating targets are cylindrical in shape and used for continuous deposition, making them ideal for high-speed processes and achieving a higher deposition rate. Both market types cater to different needs, allowing various industries to utilize nickel chromium silicon targets efficiently.</p></p>
