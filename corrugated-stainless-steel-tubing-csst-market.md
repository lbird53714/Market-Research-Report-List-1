<p><p>Corrugated Stainless Steel Tubing (CSST) refers to a flexible, durable, and corrosion-resistant piping system widely used for gas distribution in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. Its market types include 304 SS and 316 SS. The 304 SS variant is made from a combination of chromium and nickel, offering excellent resistance to corrosion and high-temperature environments. On the other hand, the 316 SS variant contains additional molybdenum, providing enhanced resistance against chemicals, acids, and chloride environments. Both market types cater to different requirements, ensuring safe and efficient gas distribution.</p></p>
