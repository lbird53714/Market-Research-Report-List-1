<p><ul><li>Thermal Oxidizer</li><li>Catalytic Oxidizer</li><li>Regenerative Thermal Oxidizer</li><li>Regenerative Catalytic Oxidizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal Treatment Air Filtration Systems refer to the equipment used to remove pollutants from industrial exhaust gases. The market is divided into four types: 1) Thermal Oxidizer, which converts pollutants into carbon dioxide and water vapor through high temperatures, 2) Catalytic Oxidizer, which uses a catalyst to achieve the same process at lower temperatures, 3) Regenerative Thermal Oxidizer, which stores and reuses the heat generated during the combustion process, and 4) Regenerative Catalytic Oxidizer, which combines the benefits of both regenerative and catalytic oxidizers. These systems play a crucial role in improving air quality and reducing harmful emissions.</p></p>
