<p><p>Graphene composites market comprises several types, including Graphene Oxide, Graphene Film, and Graphene Nanoplatelets. Graphene Film involves a thin layer of graphene deposited on a surface, possessing excellent electrical conductivity and mechanical strength. Graphene Nanoplatelets are thin sheets of graphene with high aspect ratio, enabling improved mechanical, thermal, and electrical properties when incorporated into composites. These three types of graphene composites have various applications in industries such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
