<p><p>Electric heat trace cable market includes various types of heaters, such as self-regulating heaters and constant wattage heaters. Self-regulating heaters are designed with a conductive polymer that adjusts its heat output based on the surrounding temperature, providing energy efficiency and preventing overheating. Constant wattage heaters, on the other hand, offer a fixed heat output regardless of the temperature, making them suitable for applications requiring a consistent level of heating. These market types cater to different heating requirements and are utilized in various industries including oil and gas, power generation, and residential sectors.</p></p>
