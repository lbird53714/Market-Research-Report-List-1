<p><ul><li>Single Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (SEXA)</li><li>Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (pDEXA)</li><li>Radiographic Absorptiometry (RA)</li><li>Quantitative Ultrasound (QUS)</li><li>Peripheral Quantitative Computed Tomography (pQCT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peripheral bone densitometry measurement market refers to the market for devices that assess bone health at peripheral sites. Single Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (SEXA) uses a single X-ray beam, while Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (pDEXA) employs two beams to differentiate bone and soft tissue. Radiographic Absorptiometry (RA) measures bone mineral density based on radiographs. Quantitative Ultrasound (QUS) measures bone density using sound waves. Peripheral Quantitative Computed Tomography (pQCT) assesses bone 3D structure. These technologies aid in diagnosing osteoporosis and monitoring treatment efficacy, ultimately promoting better bone health.</p></p>
