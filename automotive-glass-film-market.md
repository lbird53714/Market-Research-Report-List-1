<p><p>The automotive glass film market consists of different types of films, namely tinted film, metal film, and sputtering film. Tinted film is a colored film applied to the glass surface, providing privacy and reducing glare. Metal film contains a thin layer of metal and offers enhanced heat rejection and UV protection. Sputtering film is created using a sputtering process, which deposits multiple layers of metals and ceramics, providing excellent thermal insulation, UV protection, and durability. These films improve the performance, aesthetics, and safety of automotive glass.</p></p>
