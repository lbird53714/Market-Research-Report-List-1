<p><strong>The Glass Fiber Reinforced Polypropylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Extrusion Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass fiber reinforced polypropylene market is divided into two main types: injection molding and extrusion molding. Injection molding involves the process of injecting molten polypropylene mixed with glass fibers into a mold cavity, creating various shapes and sizes of products with excellent strength and rigidity. On the other hand, extrusion molding utilizes a continuous process where the molten polypropylene and glass fibers are pushed through a die, resulting in long shapes like pipes, tubes, and sheets. Both methods offer versatility and durability for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
