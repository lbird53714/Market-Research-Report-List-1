<p><p>ERM (Eccentric Rotating Mass) actuators and LRA (Linear Resonant Actuators) are two types of tactile actuators used in various industries. ERM actuators consist of an unbalanced mass that rotates rapidly, creating vibrations when driven by an electrical signal. These actuators are commonly used in mobile devices and gaming controllers. On the other hand, LRA actuators produce vibrations by oscillating a mass at its resonant frequency. They provide precise and responsive haptic feedback, thus finding applications in wearable technology and virtual reality devices. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing user experience by providing tactile sensations.</p></p>
