The market for these adhesives can be divided into different types: water-based, solvent-based, hot melt, and others. Water-based PSA use water as a carrier, making them environmentally friendly. Solvent-based PSA utilize organic solvents to dissolve the acrylic polymers. Hot melt PSA are solid at room temperature but turn into a liquid upon heating, providing fast bonding. Other types include UV-curable and radiation-curable PSA which offer quick curing through special processes.</p></p>
