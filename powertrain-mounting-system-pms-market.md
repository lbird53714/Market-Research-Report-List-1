<p><p>The Powertrain Mounting System (PMS) market encompasses various types, including Pure Rubber Mounting and Hydraulic Mounting. Pure Rubber Mounting is a type of powertrain mounting system that uses rubber as its primary material. It provides excellent vibration and noise isolation, making it suitable for various vehicles' powertrain applications. On the other hand, Hydraulic Mounting is a type of powertrain mounting system that utilizes hydraulic fluid to dampen vibrations and shocks. It offers better performance in terms of vibration isolation and can adapt to changing loads and conditions.</p></p>
