<p><p>Plasma surface preparation machines are used to treat surfaces before applying coatings or adhesives, enhancing the bonding process. There are two main types: atmospheric pressure plasma machines and low pressure/vacuum plasma machines. Atmospheric pressure machines operate in normal air pressure and are more cost-effective and easier to use. Low pressure/vacuum machines operate in a controlled environment with reduced air pressure, allowing for more precise and efficient surface treatment. Both types offer unique benefits and can be selected based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
