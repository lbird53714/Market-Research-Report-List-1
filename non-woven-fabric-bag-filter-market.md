<p><p>The Non-woven Fabric Bag Filter market consists of three main types: Mechanical Shaker Bag Filter, Reverse Air Bag Filter, and Pulse Jet Bag Filter. Mechanical Shaker Bag Filter operates by shaking the bags to remove the dust. Reverse Air Bag Filter utilizes reverse airflow to clean the bags. Pulse Jet Bag Filter uses high-pressure air pulses to dislodge and remove the dust from the bags. All three types are effective in filtering air pollutants but vary in their cleaning mechanisms.</p></p>
