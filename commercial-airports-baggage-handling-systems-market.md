<p><p>The Commercial Airports Baggage Handling Systems market includes two types of technologies: Barcode and RFID. Barcode technology involves the use of a series of parallel lines that represent data, which is scanned and processed to track and sort luggage efficiently. On the other hand, RFID (Radio-Frequency Identification) technology uses radio waves to identify and track baggage. These systems improve airport operational efficiency by accurately identifying and sorting luggage, reducing manual labor, and minimizing errors in baggage handling.</p></p>
