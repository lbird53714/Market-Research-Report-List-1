<p><p>Condition monitoring systems are used to monitor the health and performance of machinery in various industries. Vibration analysis and diagnostics involve measuring and analyzing machine vibrations to detect any potential issues. Ultrasound testing is used to measure material thickness and identify flaws in structures. Acoustic emission uses airborne ultrasound to detect faults in equipment. Infrared thermography measures and analyzes heat signatures to identify overheating components. Motor condition monitoring and motor current signature analysis are techniques used to monitor motor performance. Other condition monitoring systems include various other techniques like oil analysis and wear debris analysis.</p></p>
