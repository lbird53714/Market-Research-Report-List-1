<p><p>Low Temp Co-fired Ceramic (LTCC) material is used in the production of electronic components due to its high electrical and thermal conductivity. There are two types of LTCC materials: Glass-ceramic system and Amorphous Glass System. In the Glass-ceramic system, LTCC materials have a crystalline structure and are highly durable, making them suitable for applications requiring high mechanical strength. In contrast, the Amorphous Glass System consists of LTCC materials with an amorphous structure, which offers better transparency and flexibility, making it ideal for optical and sensor applications.</p></p>
