<p><p>The high density graphite market consists of various types such as isotropic graphite, extruded graphite, molded graphite, and others. Isotropic graphite possesses uniform properties in all directions, making it ideal for applications requiring high thermal conductivity. Extruded graphite is manufactured by forcing the material through a die, resulting in products with good mechanical strength. Molded graphite is made by molding a mixture of graphite powder and binders, offering flexibility and customization options. The "others" category includes specialty graphite products tailored for specific applications such as crucibles or EDM electrodes.</p></p>
