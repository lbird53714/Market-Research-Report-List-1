<p><p>Self-service kiosks for the cinema market come in two main types: wall-mounted and floor-standing. Wall-mounted self-service kiosks are installed on walls and provide a convenient way for customers to purchase tickets, select seats, and make concessions orders. They save space and are highly visible. On the other hand, floor-standing self-service kiosks are freestanding units that are often equipped with touch screens. They offer similar functionalities but are more flexible in terms of placement and can be easily moved around the cinema premises.</p></p>
