<p><p>Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) are used in various industries to protect materials from degradation caused by light exposure. The market for HALS can be categorized into three types: polymeric, monomeric, and oligomeric. Polymeric HALS are known for their excellent thermal stability and long-lasting protective properties. Monomeric HALS are single molecules that offer good compatibility with different materials and easy handling. Oligomeric HALS are a combination of several monomeric molecules, providing enhanced efficiency and stability. All three types play a crucial role in protecting materials and extending their lifespan.</p></p>
