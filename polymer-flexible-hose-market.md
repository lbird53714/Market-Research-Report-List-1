<p><p>The Polymer Flexible Hose market is segmented into three types: Thermoset Elastomer, Thermoplastic Resin, and Thermoplastic Elastomer. Thermoset Elastomer hoses are made from a material that becomes rigid and cannot be remolded after being exposed to heat and pressure. Thermoplastic Resin hoses are made from a material that can be melted and reformed multiple times without losing its properties. Thermoplastic Elastomer hoses offer a combination of the properties of thermoplastic resin and elastomer, providing flexibility, durability, and resistance to chemicals and extreme temperatures.</p></p>
