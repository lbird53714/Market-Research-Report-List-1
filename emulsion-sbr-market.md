Emulsion SBR is extensively used in tire manufacturing due to its superior grip on wet surfaces, durability, and fuel efficiency. As the automotive industry continues to grow, especially in emerging economies, the demand for Emulsion SBR is expected to witness significant growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the construction industry, particularly infrastructure development, is another key driver for the market.
