<p><p>Dental 3D scanners are used for various dental applications like digital impressions and treatment planning. The market is divided into different types based on functionality. Desktop or laboratory scanners are used in dental labs for producing highly accurate 3D models. Intraoral scanners are designed for capturing images inside the patient's mouth and are commonly used by dentists. Hand-held scanners offer mobility and flexibility during image capturing. Cone Beam Computerized Tomography (CBCT) scanners provide detailed 3D images of a patient's oral and maxillofacial region, aiding in complex dental procedures. These scanners play a crucial role in the dental industry, offering efficient and precise dental imaging solutions.</p></p>
