Cold pressed citrus oils are extracted by mechanically pressing the peel, ensuring no heat is involved in the process. This technique is often used for oils like grapefruit and lime. Other types of citrus oils may involve solvents or other extraction methods, offering niche options for different preferences and applications.</p></p>
