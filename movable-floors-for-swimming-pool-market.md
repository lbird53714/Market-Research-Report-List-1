<p><p>Movable floors for swimming pool market consists of two main types: suspended type and floating type. The suspended type is designed to be installed below the pool's water level and can be raised or lowered based on the user's preference. It allows for easy adjusting of pool depth for different activities or user preferences. On the other hand, the floating type involves movable platforms that can be placed on top of the pool water surface. They provide a versatile solution for transforming the pool into a different space, such as a dance floor or additional seating area. Both types offer flexibility and customization options for pool owners.</p></p>
