<p><p>Special rubber market types include nitrile rubber, fluorine rubber, butyl rubber, silicone rubber, acrylate rubber, and others. Nitrile rubber has excellent resistance to oil, making it suitable for applications in the automotive and oil industry. Fluorine rubber exhibits exceptional chemical resistance and is commonly used in industries requiring resistance to solvents and acids. Butyl rubber provides good impermeability to gases and is often used in applications requiring air tightness. Silicone rubber is known for its high temperature resistance and is used in diverse industries. Acrylate rubber offers good weathering and abrasion resistance.</p></p>
