<p><p>Aseptic diaphragm valves are used in various industries to maintain sterile and hygienic conditions in processes. The market for these valves can be categorized into two types: 2-way and 3-way valves. </p><p>2-way valves have two ports, which can either be open or closed to control the flow of fluids. They are commonly used to start or stop the flow, or to regulate the fluid in a single direction.</p><p>On the other hand, 3-way valves have three ports, allowing more control over the flow direction. These valves are used for diverting or mixing the flow of fluids in different directions within a system.</p></p>
