<p><p>Joule-Thomson cryocoolers are devices that are used for cooling applications in various industries such as aerospace, medical, and research. This effect is harnessed in cryocoolers to achieve low temperatures.</p><p>The current outlook of the Joule-Thomson cryocoolers market is positive and is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
