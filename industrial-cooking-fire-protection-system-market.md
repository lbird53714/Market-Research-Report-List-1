<p><p>The industrial cooking fire protection system market is categorized into two types: fire detection systems and fire management systems. Fire management systems, on the other hand, aim to control and suppress fires by using technologies like fire suppression systems, fire extinguishers, and automated fire alarms. Both systems play crucial roles in ensuring the safety of cooking operations in industrial settings.</p></p>
