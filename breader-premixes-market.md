<p><p>Breader premixes market consists of two main types: crumbs & flakes, and flour & starch. Crumbs & flakes are small particles that are used as a coating for various food products, providing a crispy and crunchy texture. They are commonly used for breading chicken, fish, and other meat products. On the other hand, flour & starch are fine powders that are mixed with other ingredients to create a thick batter for coating food items. They are typically used to make dishes like tempura or as a base for classic breaded items like chicken tenders or onion rings.</p></p>
