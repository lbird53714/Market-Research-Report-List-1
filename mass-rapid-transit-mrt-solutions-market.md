<p><p>Mass Rapid Transit (MRT) solutions are transportation systems designed to efficiently carry a large number of passengers in urban areas. Personal Rapid Transit (PRT) is an individualized form of MRT, where small automated vehicles carry a few passengers and operate on a network of dedicated tracks. On the other hand, Group Rapid Transit (GRT) is a larger form of MRT, with automated vehicles capable of carrying larger groups of passengers simultaneously. Both PRT and GRT aim to provide convenient and efficient transportation options while reducing traffic congestion and promoting sustainability in cities.</p></p>
