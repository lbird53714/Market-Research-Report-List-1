<p><p>Intrathecal drug delivery is a method of delivering medication directly into the spinal canal, bypassing the bloodstream. It involves the use of a drug pump that is surgically implanted near the spinal cord and delivers medication via a catheter to the intrathecal space surrounding the spinal cord. This method is commonly used for the treatment of chronic pain, spasticity associated with conditions such as multiple sclerosis and cerebral palsy, and cancer pain management.</p><p>The global intrathecal drug delivery market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
