<p><p>The Airbag Control Unit Sensor market encompasses two types of sensors: Mass Type Sensor and Roller Type Sensor. The Mass Type Sensor measures the car's deceleration during a crash, activating the airbag when it detects a significant decrease in speed. Both sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of passengers by facilitating the timely activation of airbags in different crash scenarios.</p></p>
