<p><p>Refinery fuel additives market types include antioxidants, cold-flow improvers, conductivity improver, dehazer, scavengers, octane improver, cetane number improver, lubricity improver, and others. Antioxidants prevent fuel oxidation, while cold-flow improvers enhance low-temperature performance. Conductivity improvers increase electrical conductivity. Dehazers help remove impurities and improve fuel clarity. Scavengers remove harmful contaminants. Octane improvers boost octane levels in gasoline, and cetane number improvers enhance diesel fuel combustion. Lubricity improvers reduce friction and wear. Other additives cater to various fuel-related needs. These additives help optimize fuel performance, improve engine efficiency, and meet regulatory requirements in the refinery fuel sector.</p></p>
