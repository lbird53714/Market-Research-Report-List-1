<p><p>Captive hydrogen refers to the production and consumption of hydrogen for specific purposes within a closed system, typically by industries that require hydrogen as a feedstock or fuel. It is not sold or traded on the open market. On the other hand, the merchant hydrogen market involves the buying and selling of hydrogen as a standalone commodity between various suppliers and customers, enabling broader distribution, competition, and market dynamics. Merchant hydrogen is often used in applications such as fuel cell vehicles, energy storage, and industrial processes.</p></p>
