<p><p>The High Barrier Vacuum Pouches market is primarily divided into three types: PA High Barrier Vacuum Pouches, EVOH High Barrier Vacuum Pouches, and PE High Barrier Vacuum Pouches. PA High Barrier Vacuum Pouches are pouches made from polyamide, offering excellent puncture resistance and oxygen barrier properties. EVOH High Barrier Vacuum Pouches incorporate ethylene vinyl alcohol copolymer, providing exceptional oxygen and aroma barrier properties. PE High Barrier Vacuum Pouches, on the other hand, are pouches made from polyethylene, known for their moisture and oxygen barrier capabilities. These different types cater to diverse packaging needs within the vacuum pouches industry.</p></p>
