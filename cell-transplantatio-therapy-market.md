<p><p>Cell transplantation therapy market includes two types: stem cell and non-stem cell. It holds great promise for treating various diseases, such as Parkinson's and Alzheimer's. Non-stem cell therapy, on the other hand, involves the transplantation of specialized cells, like skin cells or pancreatic cells, to restore specific functions.
