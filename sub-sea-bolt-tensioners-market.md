<p><p>The Sub Sea Bolt Tensioners market consists of two main types: the Single Hole Structure and the Double Hole Structure. The Single Hole Structure tensioners are designed with a single hole for bolt insertion, making them suitable for applications where space is limited. On the other hand, the Double Hole Structure tensioners have two holes, providing flexibility and allowing for bolt insertion from different angles. Both types are essential in subsea operations to ensure proper tensioning of bolts and the overall safety and efficiency of the equipment.</p></p>
