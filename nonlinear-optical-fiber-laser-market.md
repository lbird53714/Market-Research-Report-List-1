<p><p>The nonlinear optical fiber laser market consists of two main types: continuous wave (CW) fiber laser and pulsed fiber laser. Continuous wave fiber laser operates by emitting a continuous laser beam, making it suitable for applications that require a steady and continuous beam of laser light. On the other hand, pulsed fiber laser emits laser pulses at regular intervals, resulting in high peak power output. Pulsed fiber lasers are commonly used in applications such as material processing, scientific research, and telecommunications.</p></p>
