<p><p>TPEG, or Transport Protocol Experts Group, encompasses various market types. The Paste Type market refers to the collection and distribution of real-time traffic information using physical media, such as CDs or USB drives. In contrast, the Flaky Type market involves the dissemination of traffic data through ad-hoc wireless networks, where information is exchanged between vehicles. Both market types aim to provide up-to-date and reliable traffic information to users. However, Paste Type relies on physical media distribution, while Flaky Type relies on wireless connectivity between vehicles.</p></p>
