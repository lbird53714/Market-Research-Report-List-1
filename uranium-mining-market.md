<p><p>Uranium mining market types can be categorized into four main types of deposits. Granite-type uranium deposits are found in granitic rocks and are typically low in grade. Volcanic-type uranium deposits occur in volcanic rocks and are often associated with hydrothermal activity. Sandstone-type uranium deposits are found in sandstone rocks and are usually characterized by higher grades. Carbonate-siliceous-pelitic rock type uranium deposits are typically hosted in carbonate or siliceous rocks and can have variable grades. These different types of deposits vary in terms of their geology, grade, and potential for uranium extraction.</p></p>
