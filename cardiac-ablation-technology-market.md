<p><p>Cardiac ablation technology is used to treat various heart conditions by destroying targeted abnormal tissue. There are several types of cardiac ablation technologies available in the market. Radiofrequency ablation uses heat generated by radiofrequency waves to destroy abnormal tissue. Cryoablation uses extreme cold to freeze and destroy the tissue. Laser ablation employs high-intensity laser beams for tissue destruction. Ultrasound ablation uses high-energy ultrasound waves for the same purpose. Microwave ablation uses high-frequency electromagnetic waves. "Others" encompass less commonly used technologies such as hydrothermal, electroporation, and irreversible electroporation.</p></p>
