<p><strong>The CMP Slurries for Through Silicon Via Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Side Slurries</li><li>Back Side Slurries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP slurries for through silicon via (TSV) are used in semiconductor manufacturing to remove excess materials during the creation of micro-sized holes in silicon wafers. Front side slurries are applied on the front side of the wafer and help to planarize the surface, ensuring uniformity. Back side slurries, on the other hand, are used on the back side of the wafer to eliminate any roughness and improve the electrical conductivity of the TSV. These slurries play a crucial role in enabling the production of reliable and efficient TSVs in various electronic devices.</p></p>
