<p><p>Gas Cluster Ion Beams (GCIB) is a technology used for material processing and surface modification. The market for GCIB can be divided into two main types: Sputter Beams and Analytical Beams. Sputter Beams are primarily used for thin film deposition and surface cleaning, providing advantages like higher deposition rates and damage reduction. Analytical Beams, on the other hand, are used for various analytical techniques such as secondary ion mass spectrometry (SIMS) and time-of-flight secondary ion mass spectrometry (TOF-SIMS) for surface analysis and depth profiling. Both types offer unique capabilities and find applications in diverse industries.</p></p>
