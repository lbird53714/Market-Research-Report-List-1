<p><p>The Welding Fumes Processor market includes two types of processors: Single Arm Welding Fumes Processor and Dual-Arm Welding Fumes Processor. A Single Arm Welding Fumes Processor is designed to extract and filter fumes generated during welding processes efficiently. It is equipped with an extraction arm that can be positioned flexibly to capture the fumes directly at the source.
