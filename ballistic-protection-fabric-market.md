<p><ul><li>Polyethylene-based Ballistic Protection Fabric</li><li>Aramid-based (Aromatic Amide) Ballistic Protection Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ballistic protection fabric market consists of two main types: polyethylene-based and aramid-based ballistic protection fabric. Polyethylene-based fabric is made from high-density polyethylene fibers, which provide excellent strength and durability. It is known for its lightweight and flexible properties, making it suitable for applications like bulletproof vests and helmets. Aramid-based fabric, on the other hand, is made from aromatic amide fibers, which offer exceptional resistance to heat and flames. This type of fabric is commonly used in military and law enforcement applications where protection against ballistic threats and fire hazards is crucial.</p></p>
