<p><p>Plastics Packaging Film and Sheet market encompasses various types, namely PE, BOPP, and BOPET. BOPP (biaxially oriented polypropylene) film offers excellent clarity and resistance to moisture, making it ideal for food packaging. BOPET (biaxially oriented polyethylene terephthalate) film is widely used in the packaging industry due to its superior thermal stability, tensile strength, and chemical resistance. These types of films and sheets serve as essential components in the packaging industry, catering to different needs and applications.</p></p>
