<p><strong>The Bilirubin Assay Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diazonium Method</li><li>Vanadate Oxidation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bilirubin Assay Kits market includes different types of kits used for measuring bilirubin levels in the blood. The Diazonium Method is a commonly used technique that involves the reaction of bilirubin with a diazonium salt to produce an absorbent compound, which can be measured spectrophotometrically. On the other hand, the Vanadate Oxidation Method utilizes the oxidation of bilirubin by vanadate ions, forming a blue-colored product that can be quantified using a spectrophotometer. Both methods are reliable and widely used in various clinical settings.</p></p>
