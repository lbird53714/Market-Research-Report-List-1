<p><p>Natural Diatomite is a versatile mineral used in various industries. Anhydrous Substance refers to diatomite that has been processed to remove all water content, making it highly absorbent. This type is commonly used in filtration systems and as a soil conditioner. Flux Calcined diatomite has been heated to high temperatures, resulting in a more porous structure. It is primarily used in insulation, soundproofing, and as a lightweight aggregate in construction materials. Understanding these different market types helps businesses determine the most suitable applications for natural diatomite products.</p></p>
