<p><p>The Human Blood Glucose Test Strip market consists of different types of test strips that are used to measure glucose levels in the blood. One type is the Glucose Oxidase test strip, which uses the enzyme glucose oxidase to convert glucose into gluconic acid and hydrogen peroxide. Another type is the PQQ-Glucose Dehydrogenase test strip, which employs the enzyme PQQ-GDH to convert glucose into gluconolactone and produce an electric current. Additionally, there is the FAD-Glucose Dehydrogenase test strip, using the enzyme FAD-GDH, and the Hexokinase test strip, which measures glucose levels by measuring the production of NADPH. These different types cater to various needs and preferences of individuals requiring blood glucose monitoring.</p></p>
