<p><p>An electronic ballast is a device used in fluorescent lamps to regulate the flow of electrical current passing through them. It acts as a transformer, converting the voltage from the power source into the optimal voltage required to ignite and maintain the operation of the lamp. Electronic ballasts offer advantages over traditional magnetic ballasts, including higher efficiency, reduced flickering, longer lamp life, and improved light quality.</p><p>The electronic ballast market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing adoption of energy-efficient lighting solutions in residential and commercial buildings is a key driver for the market.
