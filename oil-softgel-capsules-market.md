With the rise of the flexitarian and vegetarian lifestyles, consumers are seeking alternatives to traditional fish oil capsules that are derived from animal sources. As a result, manufacturers are now offering softgel capsules containing oils from plant-based sources such as flaxseed, hempseed, or algae.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the inclusion of specialized ingredients in oil softgel capsules to target specific health concerns.
