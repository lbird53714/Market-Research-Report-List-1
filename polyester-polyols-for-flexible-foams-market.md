<p><p>Polyester polyols are a type of raw material used in the production of flexible foams. The market for these polyols is categorized by three main types of production methods: vacuum melting method, carrier gas melting method, and azeotropic vapor method. In the vacuum melting method, the polyols are melted under low pressure to remove impurities. The carrier gas melting method uses a gas to evaporate the impurities. Lastly, the azeotropic vapor method utilizes a special liquid mixture to extract impurities from the polyols. These methods ensure the production of high-quality polyester polyols for flexible foams.</p></p>
