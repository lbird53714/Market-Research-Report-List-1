<p><p>The automotive needle roller bearing market includes three main types: radial type, thrust type, and others. Radial type needle roller bearings are designed to support radial loads and have a compact design. Thrust type needle roller bearings, on the other hand, are used to handle axial loads and have a high load-carrying capacity. The "others" category includes specialized needle roller bearings used for specific automotive applications such as cam followers, track rollers, and combination bearings. Overall, these different types cater to varying load and application requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
