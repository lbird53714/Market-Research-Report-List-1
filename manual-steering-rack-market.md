Firstly, the increasing demand for light-weight and fuel-efficient vehicles has led to the adoption of manual steering racks in smaller cars and entry-level models. <p><p>The manual steering rack market can be categorized into two types: rack and pinion type and ball screw type. Rack and pinion type steering racks use a gear mechanism (pinion gear and rack) to convert rotational motion into linear motion, providing steering control. On the other hand, ball screw type steering racks utilize a ball screw mechanism to achieve linear motion, enabling precise steering control. These two types offer different steering experiences and have their own advantages and applications in the manual steering rack market.</p></p>
