<p><p>Professional safe boxes market includes two main types: mechanical safe and electric safe. Mechanical safes are traditional safes that use a mechanical lock mechanism, such as combination locks or key locks, to safeguard valuables. They are reliable and durable, but require manual operation and can be time-consuming to unlock. Electric safes, on the other hand, incorporate electronic components for locking and unlocking, utilizing technologies like keypad codes or biometric identification. These safes offer convenience and quick access, but may be more vulnerable to hacking or power failures.</p></p>
