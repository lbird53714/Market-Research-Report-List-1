<p><p>Optical lens edging devices market is segmented into two types: semi-automatic and full-automatic. Semi-automatic devices require manual intervention during the lens edging process. These devices have features like lens blocking, roughing, and polishing, requiring human monitoring. On the other hand, full-automatic devices are equipped with advanced technology and require minimal human interaction. They can complete the lens edging process independently, from blocking to polishing, making them more efficient and time-saving. Both types serve the purpose of grinding and shaping optical lenses but differ in terms of the level of automation and human involvement.</p></p>
