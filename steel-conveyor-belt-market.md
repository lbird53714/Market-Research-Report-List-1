<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Steel conveyor belt market is classified into two types: strand steel cord rubber conveyor belts and layer steel cord rubber conveyor belts. Strand steel cord rubber conveyor belts are made up of a single layer of steel cords embedded in the rubber cover, providing excellent strength and durability. On the other hand, layer steel cord rubber conveyor belts consist of multiple layers of steel cords with rubber covers, offering enhanced protection against impact and tear resistance. Both types of belts are widely used in industries for conveying heavy materials efficiently and safely.</p></p>
