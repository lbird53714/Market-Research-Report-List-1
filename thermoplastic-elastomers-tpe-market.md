<p><p>Thermoplastic elastomers (TPE) refer to a group of materials that possess both elastomeric and thermoplastic properties. They can be melted and re-molded multiple times without undergoing degradation. There are several types of TPE in the market including styrene-based TPE (SBCs), thermoplastic polyolefins (TPO), thermoplastic polyurethanes (TPU), polyether ester TPE (TPEE), and others. SBCs offer excellent flexibility and processability, TPOs provide good impact resistance and weatherability, TPUs exhibit high tensile strength and abrasion resistance, TPEEs offer improved chemical resistance, while other TPE types have unique characteristics suitable for specific applications.</p></p>
