<p><ul><li>Electrically Conductive Adhesives</li><li>Non-Electrically Conductive Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Assembly Adhesives market consists of two major types: Electrically Conductive Adhesives and Non-Electrically Conductive Adhesives. Electrically Conductive Adhesives are used to provide electrical conductivity between components in electronic devices, allowing the flow of electricity. They are commonly utilized in applications such as bonding integrated circuits, electronic displays, and surface-mount devices. On the other hand, Non-Electrically Conductive Adhesives are used for bonding and sealing electronic components, preventing the entry of moisture and protecting against environmental factors. These adhesives are widely used in the assembly of printed circuit boards and semiconductor devices.</p></p>
