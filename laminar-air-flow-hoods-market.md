<p><p>Laminar airflow hoods are used in laboratories and healthcare facilities to provide a sterile environment for handling and manipulating substances. There are two types of laminar airflow hoods: vertical and horizontal. </p><p>Vertical laminar airflow hoods have a downward airflow that protects the work surface and prevents contamination from above. They are typically used for operations that require protection from hazardous materials.</p><p>Horizontal laminar airflow hoods have a horizontal airflow that protects the operator from substances inside the hood. These hoods are commonly used for procedures that involve sensitive materials or operations that require a high level of sterility.</p></p>
