The market for these coatings can be categorized into four main types: collagen, protein, polysaccharides, and others. Collagen-based coatings are derived from animal sources and are known for providing excellent mechanical properties. Protein-based coatings, on the other hand, are mainly sourced from plant materials and offer good barrier properties. Polysaccharide coatings, such as edible starch, provide moisture resistance and improved texture. The "other" category includes coatings made from various materials like lipids, resins, and waxes, each with unique functionalities suited for specific food products.</p></p>
