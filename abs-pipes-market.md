<p><p>ABS pipes are available in various types to cater to different plumbing needs. The first type is the two-way pipe, which is a straight conduit used to connect two plumbing fixtures. The second type is a tee, which is shaped like a "T" and allows for three connections, commonly used for junctions. The third type, known as a cross pipe, has a cross-shaped design and facilitates four connections, commonly used at intersections. Lastly, the market also offers other types of ABS pipes that serve specific purposes, such as reducing couplings, end caps, and adapters.</p></p>
