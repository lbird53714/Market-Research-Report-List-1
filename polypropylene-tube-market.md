<p><p>The polypropylene tube market consists of various types, including PP-RTube, PP-R Aluminum Foiled Tube, and others. PP-RTube refers to polypropylene random copolymer tubes that offer excellent long-term performance and durability in plumbing systems. PP-R Aluminum Foiled Tube, on the other hand, includes a layer of aluminum foil sandwiched between two layers of PP-R, providing enhanced resistance to high temperatures and oxygen diffusion. The "Others" segment includes different variations of polypropylene tubes that cater to specific industry requirements, such as medical, automotive, or industrial applications.</p></p>
