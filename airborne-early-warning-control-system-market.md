<p><p>The Airborne Early Warning Control System (AEWCS) market consists of two types of systems: multispectral and hyperspectral. Multispectral systems use sensors that can detect and analyze different wavelengths of the electromagnetic spectrum, providing valuable data about various objects or environments. On the other hand, hyperspectral systems have higher spectral resolution and can capture a larger number of narrow and contiguous bands, enabling more detailed analysis of specific materials or substances. Both types of systems play a crucial role in surveillance, intelligence gathering, and monitoring activities for defense and civil applications.</p></p>
