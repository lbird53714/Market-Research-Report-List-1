<p><ul><li>Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB)</li><li>Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer carrier boxes market comprises two main types: Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB) and Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP). FOSB is used for shipping wafers in the semiconductor industry. It features a front opening design and provides protection during transportation. FOUP, on the other hand, is a more advanced version used for wafer storage. It also has a front opening mechanism but includes added features like environmental control and automation capabilities to ensure precise and safe handling of wafers during storage and handling processes.</p></p>
