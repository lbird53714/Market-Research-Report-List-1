<p><p>The greenhouse heating and cooling systems market consists of two main types: greenhouse heating systems and greenhouse cooling systems. Greenhouse heating systems are designed to maintain a warm temperature inside the greenhouse, especially during colder seasons, by utilizing different methods such as hot water pipes, forced air heaters, or radiant heaters. On the other hand, greenhouse cooling systems are used to control the temperature and humidity levels inside the greenhouse during warmer seasons. These systems include methods like natural ventilation, evaporative cooling, and mechanical ventilation.</p></p>
