<p><p>The Fluorine Doped Tin Oxide (FTO) market consists of two main types: N-Type and P-Type. N-Type FTO is doped with impurities that provide it with an excess of electrons, making it highly conductive. It is commonly used in applications such as solar cells and transparent conductive coatings on glass. On the other hand, P-Type FTO is doped with impurities that create electron "holes," which makes it a highly conductive material for various electronic devices. Both N-Type and P-Type FTO play significant roles in the development of advanced technologies.</p></p>
