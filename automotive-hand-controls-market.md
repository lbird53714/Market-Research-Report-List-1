<p><strong>The Automotive Hand Controls Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push–Pull Hand Controls</li><li>Push Right Angle Hand Controls</li><li>Push–Rock Hand Controls</li><li>Push–Twist Hand Controls</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Hand Controls Market offers different types of hand controls to assist individuals with limited mobility in operating vehicles. Push-Pull Hand Controls allow users to push the lever forward to apply gas and pull it back to apply brakes. Push Right Angle Hand Controls enable users to push the lever sideways to control acceleration and brakes. Push-Rock Hand Controls operate similar to a seesaw, allowing users to push forward to accelerate and rock back to brake. Lastly, Push-Twist Hand Controls combine a twisting motion with forward pushing to control acceleration and braking.</p></p>
