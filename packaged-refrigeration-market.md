<p><p>The Packaged Refrigeration market consists of various types of systems, including the Double Pipe System and the Three Pipe System. In a Double Pipe System, two separate pipes are used for the refrigerant, with one pipe carrying the liquid refrigerant and the other pipe carrying the vapor refrigerant. This allows for efficient heat transfer. On the other hand, the Three Pipe System utilizes three pipes, one for liquid refrigerant, one for gas refrigerant, and one for oil return. This system enables effective cooling and lubrication of the compressor.</p></p>
