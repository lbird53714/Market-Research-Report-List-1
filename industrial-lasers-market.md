<p><strong>The Industrial Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CO2 Laser</li><li>Solid State Laser</li><li>Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial lasers market comprises three main types: CO2 lasers, solid-state lasers, and fiber lasers. CO2 lasers use gas as the medium to generate laser beams, making them suitable for cutting, welding, and engraving various materials. Solid-state lasers employ solid-crystal materials and are commonly used in metal cutting and welding applications due to their high power and precision. Fiber lasers, on the other hand, utilize optical fibers as the gain medium, providing high-quality beams that are ideal for marking, engraving, and cutting metals, plastics, and ceramics.</p></p>
