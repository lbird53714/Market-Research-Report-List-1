<p><p>Aerial lift trucks are vehicles equipped with lifting platforms used to elevate workers or materials. There are three main types in the market: scissor lifts, boom lifts, and telehandlers. Scissor lifts have interlocking, crisscrossing supports that vertically extend the platform. Boom lifts have extendable arms that reach out and up, allowing access to elevated areas. Telehandlers have a telescopic boom that can extend both horizontally and vertically and are mainly used for moving materials. Each type has unique features and applications but ultimately serve the purpose of providing safe, elevated access.</p></p>
