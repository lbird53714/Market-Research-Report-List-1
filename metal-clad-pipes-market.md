<p><p>Metal clad pipes are a type of composite pipe that consists of a metal outer layer and a polymer or composite inner layer. The outer metal layer provides strength and durability, while the inner layer provides chemical resistance and corrosion protection. These pipes are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, power generation, and water treatment.</p><p>The metal clad pipes market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. The increasing demand for corrosion-resistant pipes in various industries is a major driving factor for the market. Metal clad pipes offer superior corrosion resistance compared to traditional metal pipes, making them suitable for harsh operating environments. The steady growth in infrastructure development in emerging economies, particularly in the oil and gas industry, is also expected to contribute to the market growth.</p><p>Additionally, government regulations regarding environmental protection and worker safety are driving the demand for metal clad pipes. These pipes are designed to handle high-pressure and high-temperature applications, reducing the risk of leakages and accidents in industrial processes.</p><p>Furthermore, the rapid growth of the oil and gas industry, coupled with increasing investments in renewable energy sources, is expected to create lucrative opportunities for the metal clad pipes market. The pipe's ability to withstand extreme conditions and provide efficient fluid transportation makes it a preferred choice for various energy sectors.</p><p>Overall, with the increasing demand for corrosion-resistant pipes and the growth of key industries, the metal clad pipes market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
