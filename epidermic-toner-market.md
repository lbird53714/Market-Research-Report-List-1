<p><p>Epidermic toner is available in two different market types – powder and liquid. Powder toners come in a dry form and need to be mixed with water or another liquid before application. They are often packaged in containers with a shaker or dispenser. Liquid toners, on the other hand, are ready-to-use formulations that can be applied directly to the skin using a cotton pad or by spraying. Both powder and liquid toners serve the purpose of balancing the skin's pH levels, tightening pores, and removing any residue or impurities after cleansing.</p></p>
