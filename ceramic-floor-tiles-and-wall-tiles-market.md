<p><p>The Ceramic Floor Tiles and Wall Tiles market comprises two main types: glazed ceramic tiles and unglazed ceramic tiles. Glazed ceramic tiles are coated with a layer of glaze on top, providing them with a smooth and shiny finish. These tiles are highly durable, resistant to stains, and easier to clean. On the other hand, unglazed ceramic tiles do not have a glaze layer and therefore possess a more natural, matte appearance. These tiles are generally rougher in texture and are suitable for areas requiring more slip resistance.</p></p>
