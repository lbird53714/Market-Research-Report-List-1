<p><ul><li>Salient Pole Type</li><li>Smooth Cylindrical Type</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle alternator market comprises two main types: salient pole type and smooth cylindrical type. Salient pole type alternators are characterized by protruding poles on the rotor, which create a larger air gap between the rotor and stator, resulting in higher power output and enhanced cooling. On the other hand, smooth cylindrical type alternators have a cylindrical rotor without protruding poles, offering improved efficiency, reduced noise, and enhanced durability. These alternators cater to the requirements of passenger vehicles, providing reliable electrical power for various functions.</p></p>
