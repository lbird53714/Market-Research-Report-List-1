<p><p>The brake systems market consists of two main types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and Aftermarket. OEM brake systems are installed in vehicles during their initial manufacturing process. They are designed and produced by the vehicle's original manufacturer, ensuring high quality and compatibility. On the other hand, aftermarket brake systems are produced by third-party manufacturers and are available for purchase after the vehicle's purchase. These aftermarket brake systems offer alternative options to replace or upgrade the original brake system, providing customers with different specs, materials, and pricing choices.</p></p>
