<p><p>Gyroplanes are categorized into two types based on their engine types: 2-stroke and 4-stroke gyroplanes. The 2-stroke gyroplanes are powered by engines that complete a combustion cycle in two strokes of the piston, providing high power-to-weight ratio and simplicity in design. On the other hand, 4-stroke gyroplanes are equipped with engines that require four strokes of the piston for each combustion cycle, offering better fuel efficiency, smoother operation, and reduced emissions. These differing engine types present gyroplane buyers with options based on their preferences and requirements.</p></p>
