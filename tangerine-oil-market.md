Blends, as the name suggests, are mixtures of tangerine oil with other essential oils or carriers, creating unique fragrances or enhancing therapeutic properties. The "others" market refers to various tangerine oil products that do not fit into the previous categories, such as diluted versions, oil-infused cosmetics, or tangerine oil used as a flavoring agent.</p></p>
