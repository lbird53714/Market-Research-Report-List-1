<p><ul><li>Talc Lump</li><li>Talc Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Talc market can be divided into two main types: talc lump and talc powder. Talc lump refers to the raw form of talc that is extracted from mines and contains impurities. It is commonly used in industries like ceramics and fertilizers. Talc powder, on the other hand, is finely ground talc that undergoes a process of purification. It is widely used in various industries such as cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and paints for its desirable properties like smoothness, moisture absorption, and lubrication. These two forms cater to different needs and applications within the market.</p></p>
