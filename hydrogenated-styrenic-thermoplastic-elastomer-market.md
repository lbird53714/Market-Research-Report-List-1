<p><p>Hydrogenated Styrenic Thermoplastic Elastomer (HSTPE) is available in two market types: Acid Modified Type and Amine Modified Type. </p><p>The Acid Modified Type of HSTPE is created by introducing acid group modifiers into the styrenic thermoplastic elastomer. This modification enhances its barrier and adhesion properties, making it suitable for applications such as automotive parts and packaging materials that require excellent resistance to chemicals and solvents.</p><p>On the other hand, the Amine Modified Type incorporates amine group modifiers, improving its flexibility and elongation capabilities. This type of HSTPE finds applications in industries like footwear and consumer goods, where exceptional elasticity and durability are essential.</p></p>
