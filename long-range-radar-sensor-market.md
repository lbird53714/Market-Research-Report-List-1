<p><p>A long-range radar sensor is a type of sensor technology that is capable of detecting objects or targets over a long distance. Long-range radar sensors are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, defense, and maritime.</p><p>The global long-range radar sensor market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.3% during the forecast period. Long-range radar sensors are increasingly being integrated into vehicles to enable features such as adaptive cruise control, blind-spot detection, and collision avoidance systems.</p><p>In terms of technology, the market is witnessing a shift towards the development of advanced radar sensor technologies such as millimeter-wave radar and solid-state radar. <p><p>The Long Range Radar Sensor market consists of two main types - Long Range Millimeter Wave Radar Sensor and Long Range Laser Radar Sensor. The Long Range Millimeter Wave Radar Sensor uses electromagnetic waves with a frequency range of 30 to 300 GHz, providing highly accurate object detection and tracking capabilities even in harsh weather conditions.
