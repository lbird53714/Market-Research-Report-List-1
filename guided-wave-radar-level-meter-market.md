<p><p>The Guided Wave Radar Level Meter market consists of two main types: High Frequency Radar Level Gauge and Low Frequency Radar Level Gauge. High Frequency Radar Level Gauge utilizes shorter wavelengths for measuring liquid or solid levels, making it ideal for applications with shorter measurement ranges and turbulent conditions. On the other hand, Low Frequency Radar Level Gauge uses longer wavelengths, enabling it to measure levels in larger tanks or vessels with foamy liquids and steam. Both types offer accurate and reliable level measurement solutions for various industrial processes.</p></p>
