<p><ul><li>Porous Membrane</li><li>Homogeneous Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas film market types include porous membrane and homogeneous membrane. Porous membrane refers to a porous material that allows gas molecules to pass through while retaining other substances. This type is commonly used in applications such as gas separation, filtration, and purification. Homogeneous membrane, on the other hand, is a non-porous material that allows gas molecules to permeate through its structure. It is often used for applications such as gas sensors, fuel cells, and catalytic membranes. Both types have specific advantages and are tailored to meet various industry needs for gas separation and transport.</p></p>
