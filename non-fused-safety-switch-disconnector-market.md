They are commonly used in industrial applications and provide a reliable and safe disconnection. On the other hand, knife-blade switches incorporate a blade-like mechanism to open or close the electrical circuit. They are often chosen for their ease of installation, compact design, and ability to handle high currents. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring electrical safety by disconnecting power when necessary.</p></p>
