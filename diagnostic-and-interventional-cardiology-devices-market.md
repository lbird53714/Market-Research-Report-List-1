<p><p>The Diagnostic and Interventional Cardiology Devices market is divided into different types of devices used in diagnosing and treating heart conditions. Transcatheter Angiography Devices are used to visualize blood vessels, while Intravascular Ultrasound devices provide detailed images of the inside of blood vessels. Guide Wire-Based Intravascular Stenosis Assessment or Fractional Flow Reserve Devices measure blood flow and identify blockages. Intracardiac Echocardiography Devices use sound waves to create images of the heart. Optical Coherence Tomography Devices produce high-resolution images of blood vessels. Robotic-Assisted Surgical Devices help in performing minimally invasive cardiac surgeries.</p></p>
