<p><p>PVA roller brushes and sheet brushes both fall under the market types of roll shape and sheet shape, depending on their physical form. Roll shape brushes are cylindrical in shape, often attached to a handle, and are used for applying PVA (polyvinyl acetate)-based paints, adhesives, or coatings onto surfaces. On the other hand, sheet shape brushes have a flat, rectangular form and are typically used for spreading PVA over larger areas or surfaces. These different market types cater to various application needs in industries such as construction, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
