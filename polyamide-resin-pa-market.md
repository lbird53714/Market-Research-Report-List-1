<p><p>Polyamide resin (PA) is a type of synthetic polymer commonly used in the production of various products. The market for PA can be classified into two types: reactive type and nonreactive type. Reactive type PA refers to resins that require a crosslinking agent to cure, forming a three-dimensional network structure. These resins are widely used in coatings, adhesives, and composite materials. On the other hand, nonreactive type PA does not require a curing agent and is commonly used in the production of fibers, films, and molded parts.</p></p>
