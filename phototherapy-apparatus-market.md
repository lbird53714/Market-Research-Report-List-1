<p><p>The phototherapy apparatus market can be categorized into three types: conventional, LED, and fiberoptic. Conventional phototherapy apparatus uses traditional light sources like fluorescent tubes to emit a specific wavelength of light for treating conditions like jaundice in newborns. LED phototherapy apparatus employs light-emitting diodes to produce a single or narrow spectrum of light, offering better control and efficiency. Fiberoptic phototherapy apparatus utilizes fiber optic cables to deliver light directly to the patient, enabling targeted treatment and minimizing interference. Each type provides distinct advantages and considerations for various medical applications.</p></p>
