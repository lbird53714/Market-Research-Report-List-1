<p><p>The OEM insulation market comprises two main types: foamed plastics and mineral wool. Foamed plastics insulation is a lightweight, cost-effective solution made from materials like polystyrene or polyurethane foam. It offers excellent thermal insulation and is widely used in various industries. On the other hand, mineral wool insulation is made from natural or synthetic fibers and has great fire resistance properties. It is commonly used in applications where fire safety is crucial, such as in buildings or industrial settings. Both types play a vital role in providing efficient insulation for OEM applications.</p></p>
