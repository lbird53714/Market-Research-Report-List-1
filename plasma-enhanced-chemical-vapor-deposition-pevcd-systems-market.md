<p><p>Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PEVCD) Systems are used in the semiconductor industry for the deposition of thin films on substrates. There are two main types: Parallel Plate Type PECVD Systems and Tube Type PECVD Systems. </p><p>Parallel Plate Type PECVD Systems use parallel electrodes to generate a plasma above the substrate, ensuring a uniform deposition of the film. Tube Type PECVD Systems, on the other hand, use a tubular reactor where the substrate is placed inside the tube, and the plasma is generated around it. These systems provide different advantages and are used in various applications within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
