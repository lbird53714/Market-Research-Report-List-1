<p><p>The pressure isolation valves market includes various types of valves such as ball valves, butterfly valves, knife gate valves, plug valves, and others. Ball valves are round with a floating or trunnion-mounted ball that controls the flow. Butterfly valves have a rotating disc that regulates the flow with a quarter-turn motion. Knife gate valves have a sharp-edged gate to block or allow the flow of thick fluids or slurries. Plug valves use a cylindrical or conical plug to control the flow. The market also comprises other types of valves that serve specific purposes in isolating pressure in different industrial applications.</p></p>
