<p><p>Auto-dimming mirrors are devices used in vehicles to reduce glare from headlights of vehicles approaching from behind. There are two main types of auto-dimming mirrors: mechanical and electrodynamic. Mechanical auto-dimming mirrors use a photosensor that detects light and mechanical actuators to adjust the mirror's reflective surface. On the other hand, electrodynamic auto-dimming mirrors use an electrochromic gel that changes its light-blocking properties in response to an electric current, adjusting the brightness of the mirror. Both types serve the same purpose of enhancing safety by improving visibility for drivers.</p></p>
