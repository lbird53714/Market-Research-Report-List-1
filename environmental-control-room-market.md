<p><ul><li>Temperature-controlled</li><li>Humidity-controlled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The environmental control room market includes two main types: temperature-controlled and humidity-controlled. Temperature-controlled rooms maintain a consistent temperature level to meet specific requirements. These rooms are crucial in industries like pharmaceuticals where temperature-sensitive products are stored. Humidity-controlled rooms, on the other hand, regulate the moisture content in the air. They play a crucial role in industries like food processing and storage, as well as museums and archives to preserve sensitive artifacts. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal conditions for various applications requiring specific temperature or humidity levels.</p></p>
