<p><p>The Automotive Wheel Motor market consists of two main types: Inner-rotor Motor and External-rotor Motor. Both types are used to power the wheels of automobiles. Inner-rotor Motors have the rotor positioned inside the stator and are compact, high-speed, and efficient. On the other hand, External-rotor Motors have the rotor positioned outside the stator and are known for their high torque and power density. These types of motors play a crucial role in providing the necessary power for electric and hybrid vehicles to move.</p></p>
