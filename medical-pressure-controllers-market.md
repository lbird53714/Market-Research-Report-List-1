<p><p>Medical pressure controllers market comprises two main types: tube type and cuff type. </p><p>Tube type controllers consist of a pressure sensor connected to a control valve via a tube. They accurately regulate pressure by adjusting the valve opening, ensuring precise delivery to the patient. </p><p>Cuff type controllers, on the other hand, utilize an inflatable cuff placed around an organ or limb. The cuff inflates to a specific pressure, and then the controller maintains it at that level. Cuff type controllers are commonly used in blood pressure monitoring and automatic cuff inflation devices.</p></p>
