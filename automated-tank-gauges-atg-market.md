<p><p>Automated Tank Gauges (ATGs) are devices used to monitor fuel levels in storage tanks. The market for ATGs can be categorized into different types based on their operation. Float-operated tank gauges use a float connected to a measuring tape, providing accurate level readings. Servo-operated tank gauges use a servo motor to control a measuring tape's movement. Radar tank gauges utilize radar technology to measure the tank's contents. Hydrostatic tank gauges rely on pressure sensors to determine fuel levels. Other tank gauge types include ultrasonic, magnetostrictive, and thermal gauges.</p></p>
