<p><p>The primary button cell market is divided into different types based on the chemical composition of their electrodes. Alkaline button cells (LR) use alkaline electrolytes and are commonly used in low-power devices. Silver oxide button cells (SR) are known for their high energy density and are suitable for applications like watches and calculators. Lithium button cells (CR) offer long-lasting performance and are often found in devices like cameras and medical equipment. Apart from these three major types, there are other button cell types, each designed for specific requirements within various electronic devices.</p></p>
