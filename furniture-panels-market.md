<p><strong>The Furniture Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Particleboard (PB)</li><li>Medium density fibreboard (MDF)</li><li>High density fibreboard (HDF)</li><li>Oriented strand board (OSB)</li><li>Plywood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The furniture panels market consists of different types of materials used for manufacturing furniture. Particleboard (PB) is made from wood chips and resins, offering a cost-effective option. Medium density fibreboard (MDF) is made from wood fibers and offers a smoother surface. High density fibreboard (HDF) is more durable and moisture-resistant than MDF. Oriented strand board (OSB) is composed of compressed wood strands and offers structural strength. Plywood is made from thin layers of wood veneer bonded together, providing high strength and stiffness. These materials cater to different furniture manufacturing needs based on cost, strength, and surface quality requirements.</p></p>
