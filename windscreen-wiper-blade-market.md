<p><p>The windscreen wiper blade market can be categorized into three types: boneless, bone, and hybrid blades. Boneless blades, also known as frameless blades, have a sleek design and are made of a single piece of rubber, providing quieter and cleaner wiping. Bone blades have a metal frame that holds the rubber blade in place, providing durability and strength. Hybrid blades combine the features of both boneless and bone blades, offering a quieter operation and improved performance in different weather conditions.</p></p>
