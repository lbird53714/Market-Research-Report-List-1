<p><p>The market for wireless tire monitors can be broadly classified into two types: Built-in and External. Built-in tire monitors are integrated into the vehicle's existing systems and provide real-time tire pressure and temperature information directly to the vehicle's dashboard display. External tire monitors, on the other hand, are separate devices that can be attached to the tire valve stems or mounted onto the vehicle's wheel. These external monitors also provide tire pressure and temperature readings, but they typically require a separate display unit or smartphone app for monitoring.</p></p>
