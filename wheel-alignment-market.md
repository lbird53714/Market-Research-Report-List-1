<p><ul><li>Thrust Alignment</li><li>Two-Wheel Alignment</li><li>Four-Wheel Alignment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheel alignment market consists of three main types: thrust alignment, two-wheel alignment, and four-wheel alignment. Thrust alignment is a cost-effective option that aligns only the front wheels based on their thrust angle. Two-wheel alignment aligns only the front wheels and is usually performed on older or budget vehicles. Four-wheel alignment, the most common type, aligns all four wheels and is recommended for modern vehicles with independent suspension systems. These types cater to different needs and budgets, allowing customers to choose the alignment service that suits their requirements.</p></p>
