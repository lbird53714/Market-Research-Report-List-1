On the other hand, cable blowing lubricants are used for the installation of cables through ducts or conduits. They help to reduce friction and increase the flowability of the cable during installation, ensuring smooth and efficient operations. Both types of lubricants play a crucial role in the successful installation and maintenance of cables in various industries.</p></p>
