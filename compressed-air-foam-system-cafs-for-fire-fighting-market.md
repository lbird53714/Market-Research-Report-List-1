The medium-expansion foam system produces a thicker foam layer for controlling fire in larger areas like warehouses or aircraft hangars. The high-expansion foam system generates a high volume of foam with significant expansion, ideal for situations where fire needs to be quickly suppressed in open spaces or fuel storage areas.</p></p>
