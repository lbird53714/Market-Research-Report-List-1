<p><strong>The Polyurethane Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PPG Polyurethane Wheels</li><li>PTMEG Polyurethane Wheels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane Wheels are divided into two main types: PPG Polyurethane Wheels and PTMEG Polyurethane Wheels. PPG (Polypropylene Glycol) Polyurethane Wheels are made by combining PPG with diisocyanates. These wheels offer excellent load-bearing capabilities, high resilience, and resistance to chemicals and abrasion. On the other hand, PTMEG (Polytetramethylene Ether Glycol) Polyurethane Wheels are produced by combining PTMEG with diisocyanates. These wheels provide exceptional low-temperature performance, high tensile strength, and excellent abrasion resistance. Both types of wheels find application in various industries, including automotive, manufacturing, and material handling.</p></p>
