<p><p>The Aircraft and Aerospace Die Casting Components Market includes two types of die casting processes: hot die casting and cold die casting. Hot die casting involves injecting molten metal into a die cavity at high temperatures, resulting in faster solidification and excellent surface finish. On the other hand, cold die casting utilizes chilled metal to fill the die cavity at lower temperatures, allowing for better dimensional accuracy and tighter tolerances. Both processes play a crucial role in manufacturing aircraft and aerospace components, contributing to their functionality and performance.</p></p>
