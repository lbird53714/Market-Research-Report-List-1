<p><p>The veterinary imaging equipment market encompasses various types of imaging systems. Radiography (X-Ray) systems are used to capture images of the internal structures of animals. Ultrasound imaging systems employ high-frequency sound waves to visualize internal organs and tissues. Computed Tomography (CT) imaging systems use multiple X-ray images to create detailed cross-sectional images. Video Endoscopy imaging systems involve inserting a small camera into an animal's body to examine internal organs or tissues. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) systems use magnetic fields and radio waves to generate detailed images of animals' bodies.</p></p>
