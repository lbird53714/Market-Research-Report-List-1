<p><ul><li>Homopolymer Type</li><li>Copolymer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Maleic Anhydride Grafted Polypropylene (MAPP) market can be categorized into two types: Homopolymer Type and Copolymer Type. The Homopolymer Type refers to MAPP where the grafted maleic anhydride is solely attached to the polypropylene chains, resulting in enhanced adhesion and compatibility with various materials. On the other hand, Copolymer Type MAPP involves copolymerization of maleic anhydride with other monomers, which improves the blend compatibility with different polymers. Both types are widely used in industries like automotive, packaging, and construction due to their improved properties and versatility.</p></p>
