<p><p>Medical print label market types include wet glued labels, pressure-sensitive/self-adhesive labels, liner-less labels, multi-part barcode labels, in-mold labels, and shrink sleeve labels. Wet glued labels are affixed using a wet adhesive, while pressure-sensitive/self-adhesive labels stick when pressure is applied. Liner-less labels lack a liner backing, enhancing efficiency. Multi-part barcode labels allow for multiple copies of the same label for record-keeping. In-mold labels are applied during the manufacturing process, ensuring durability. Shrink sleeve labels are heat-shrunk onto containers, offering 360-degree coverage. These various types cater to different medical packaging requirements.</p></p>
