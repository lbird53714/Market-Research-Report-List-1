<p><p>The OTC Test Device market consists of various types, including lateral flow assays, immunoassays, and dipstick tests. Lateral flow assays utilize a strip with target-specific antibodies to detect the presence of a specific analyte. Immunoassays use antibodies to identify and measure the concentration of specific substances in a sample. Dipstick tests involve dipping a strip into a sample to detect the presence or absence of a particular substance. These different types offer convenient and accessible options for individuals to conduct various medical tests without the need for professional assistance.</p></p>
