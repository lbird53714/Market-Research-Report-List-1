<p><p>The digital I-O module market consists of two primary types: wired and wireless. Wired digital I-O modules require physical connections through cables to transmit information between devices. They provide reliable and secure data transfer, making them suitable for applications where stability is crucial. In contrast, wireless digital I-O modules utilize wireless communication technologies like Wi-Fi or Bluetooth to transmit data without the need for physical connections. They offer flexibility and scalability, making them suitable for applications where mobility and easy installation are important factors.</p></p>
