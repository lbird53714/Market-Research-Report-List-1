<p><p>The bulk material rotary valve market consists of three main types: electrically-actuated, pneumatically-actuated, and others. Electrically-actuated rotary valves are operated using an electric motor, providing precise and controlled handling of bulk materials. Pneumatically-actuated rotary valves use compressed air or gas to control the valve movement, allowing for fast and efficient material flow. The "Others" category includes alternative actuation methods such as hydraulic or manual operation. These different types cater to various industrial applications and provide flexibility in bulk material handling processes.</p></p>
