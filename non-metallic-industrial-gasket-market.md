<p><p>The non-metallic industrial gasket market includes various types such as spiral wound gaskets, ring joint gaskets, corrugated gaskets, and others. Spiral wound gaskets are made by winding metal and non-metallic materials together, providing excellent sealing performance. Ring joint gaskets are used in high-pressure applications and feature a metal ring that provides a tight seal. Corrugated gaskets have a flexible structure and can accommodate flange surface irregularities. Other types of gaskets include solid gaskets, jacketed gaskets, and composite gaskets, among others, each offering specific benefits for industrial applications.</p></p>
