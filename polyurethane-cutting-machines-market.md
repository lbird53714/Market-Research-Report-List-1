<p><p>Polyurethane cutting machines are available in two main market types: CNC and manually-controlled machines. CNC (Computer Numerical Control) machines are automated and operate through computer programming, allowing precise and complex cutting operations. On the other hand, manually-controlled machines are operated by human operators who control the cutting process manually. While CNC machines offer higher accuracy and efficiency, manually-controlled machines provide flexibility and are preferred for simpler cutting tasks or in situations where customization and adaptability are required.</p></p>
