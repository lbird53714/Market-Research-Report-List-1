<p><p>Metal glue, also known as metal adhesive or metal bonding adhesive, is a type of adhesive specifically designed for bonding metal surfaces together. Adoption of Hybrid Technologies: Hybrid metal glues that combine the benefits of different adhesive technologies, such as epoxy and polyurethane, are gaining popularity.
