<p><p>The Single-Use Chromatography Market includes various types of chromatography techniques used in biopharmaceutical manufacturing. Ion Exchange Membrane Chromatography involves the separation of biomolecules based on their charge properties. Affinity Membrane Chromatography selectively captures specific molecules by utilizing reversible binding interactions. Hydrophobic Interaction Membrane Chromatography distinguishes biomolecules by their hydrophobicity. These techniques enable efficient purification of biomolecules using single-use, disposable chromatography columns, offering advantages of reduced risk of cross-contamination and increased flexibility in bioprocessing operations.</p></p>
