<p><p>Thrust ball bearings are categorized into two types: single-direction bearings and double-direction bearings. Single-direction bearings, as the name suggests, can support axial loads in one direction only. On the other hand, double-direction bearings are designed to support axial loads in both directions. These bearings are commonly used in applications where high axial loads need to be supported, such as in automotive, industrial, and aerospace industries. Both types of bearings play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient rotational movement in various machines and equipment.</p></p>
