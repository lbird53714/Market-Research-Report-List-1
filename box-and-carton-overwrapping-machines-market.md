<p><p>The box and carton overwrapping machines market can be classified into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. In the manual type, operators manually feed the boxes or cartons into the machine and manually wrap them. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention, such as feeding the boxes, but the wrapping process is automated. Automatic machines are fully automated, where the entire process from feeding to wrapping is done automatically. These machines cater to various industries' needs depending on their production volume and level of automation required.</p></p>
