<p><p>Pedal Electric Cycle (PED) market types can be classified into pedal-assisted and throttle-assisted types. Pedal-assisted PEDs rely on human power through pedaling, with an electric motor offering assistance when needed. This type promotes physical exertion and a more traditional cycling experience. On the other hand, throttle-assisted PEDs can be propelled solely by the electric motor, allowing users to control the speed with a throttle without the need for pedaling. These types offer a more effortless riding experience, suitable for individuals seeking convenience and reduced physical effort.</p></p>
