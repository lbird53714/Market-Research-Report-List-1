<p><p>Remote Monitoring and Management (RMM) software is used by businesses to monitor and manage their IT infrastructure and systems remotely. RMM software is available in two types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise RMM software is installed and hosted locally within the organization's own servers and infrastructure. It provides control and customization but requires maintenance and upgrades. In contrast, Cloud-Based RMM software is hosted on external servers and accessed through the internet. It offers scalability, flexibility, and automatic updates, but may have limited customization options. Both types have their own advantages and limitations, so businesses choose based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
