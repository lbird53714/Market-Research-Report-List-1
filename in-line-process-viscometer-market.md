<p><p>The in-line process viscometer market encompasses various types based on their operating principles. Rotational viscometers measure viscosity by rotating a spindle inside a sample fluid. Torsional oscillation viscometers measure the torque required to twist a vibrating element submerged in a fluid. Vibration-based viscometers determine viscosity by analyzing the vibration characteristics of a vibrating element. Moving piston viscometers measure viscosity by measuring the resistance encountered by a moving piston in a sample fluid. Other types include coriolis, dynamic fluid pressure, acoustic wave (solid-state), and various innovative models that utilize alternative principles for viscosity measurement.</p></p>
