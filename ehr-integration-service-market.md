<p><p>EHR Integration Service market includes two types: Local Hosting and Cloud Based. Local Hosting refers to the setup where the electronic health record (EHR) system is installed and managed on the healthcare provider's own servers within their premises. This offers greater control and customization but requires substantial infrastructure and maintenance costs. On the other hand, Cloud Based EHR Integration Service enables healthcare providers to access and manage their EHR system over the internet through a remote server. This eliminates the need for local infrastructure and provides scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
