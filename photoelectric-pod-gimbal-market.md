<p><p>A 2-axis gimbal is a type of photoelectric pod gimbal that provides stabilization along two axes, typically pitch and roll. It helps capture smooth and steady footage by reducing unwanted movements in those directions. </p><p>A 3-axis gimbal, on the other hand, offers stabilization along three axes - pitch, roll, and yaw. This allows for even greater stability and eliminates unwanted movements in all directions.
