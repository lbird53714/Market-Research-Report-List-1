<p><p>The hybrid heat pump market consists of two main types: compression heat pump and absorption heat pump. Compression heat pumps use a mechanical system to compress and expand refrigerant, transferring heat between indoors and outdoors. Absorption heat pumps, on the other hand, use a different process involving a refrigerant-absorbent combination to accomplish the heat transfer. Both types are used in various applications such as residential, commercial, and industrial settings to provide efficient and environmentally friendly heating and cooling solutions.</p></p>
