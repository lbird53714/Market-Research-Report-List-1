There are different types of GDI systems available in the market. Turbulent Jet Injection (TJI) systems utilize a high-pressure pump to inject fuel turbulently into the combustion chamber. Piezoelectric fuel injectors use piezoelectric crystals to precisely control the fuel injection process. Water injection systems inject a fine mist of water into the intake air to cool the combustion temperature and reduce engine knock. These systems enhance the overall performance and efficiency of gasoline engines.</p></p>
