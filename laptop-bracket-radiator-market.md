<p><p>The laptop bracket radiator market is comprised of three main types: active laptop bracket radiator, passive laptop bracket radiator, and multi-purpose laptop bracket radiator. Active laptop bracket radiators utilize fans to actively cool the laptop, offering better heat dissipation and improved performance. Passive laptop bracket radiators, on the other hand, rely on natural convection to cool the laptop and do not require any power source. Multi-purpose laptop bracket radiators are versatile and can serve as both laptop stands and cooling systems, providing ergonomic benefits along with efficient heat dissipation.</p></p>
