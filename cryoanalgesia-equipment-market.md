<p><p>Cryoanalgesia equipment refers to devices used for the application of cold therapy to relieve pain and inflammation. The market can be classified into two types: the whole body cryo market and the local cryo market. Whole body cryo involves exposing the entire body to extreme cold temperatures, typically between -200°F to -300°F, for a short duration of time. Local cryo, on the other hand, targets specific areas of the body with cold therapy. Both types aim to provide pain relief, reduce inflammation, and enhance recovery.</p></p>
