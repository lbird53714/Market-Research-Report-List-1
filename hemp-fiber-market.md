<p><strong>The Hemp Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long (bast) Fibers</li><li>Short (core) Fibers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hemp fiber market consists of two main types: long (bast) fibers and short (core) fibers. Long fibers, found in the outer part of the hemp stalk, are durable and strong, making them ideal for manufacturing textiles, rope, and paper. On the other hand, short fibers, found in the inner part of the stalk, are softer and less durable, but are used for insulation, animal bedding, and biofuels. Both types have their own specific market applications, catering to industries that require different levels of strength and durability.</p></p>
