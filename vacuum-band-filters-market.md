<p><p>Vacuum band filters are categorized into two types: vertical vacuum band filters and horizontal vacuum band filters. Vertical vacuum band filters are designed with a vertically oriented conveyor belt that facilitates the continuous filtration of liquids and solids. On the other hand, horizontal vacuum band filters feature a horizontally oriented conveyor belt and are commonly used for dewatering applications. Both types of filters are utilized in various industries such as mining, chemical, and food processing to separate solid particles from liquids efficiently and continuously.</p></p>
