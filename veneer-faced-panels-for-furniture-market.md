MDF faced panels, on the other hand, are made by compressing wood fibers with resin, resulting in a denser and more stable material. MDF faced panels offer better screw-holding capacity and smoother surfaces, making them ideal for high-end furniture where aesthetics and durability are crucial.</p></p>
