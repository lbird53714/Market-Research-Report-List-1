<p><p>Dough Sheeter Machines are used in bakeries and food production facilities to automate the process of rolling out dough. Tabletop dough sheeters are compact and suitable for smaller-scale operations, allowing users to easily roll out dough on a countertop. Floor dough sheeters, on the other hand, are larger and more powerful, designed for high-volume production. They are often used in large bakeries and industrial settings to efficiently process large quantities of dough.</p></p>
