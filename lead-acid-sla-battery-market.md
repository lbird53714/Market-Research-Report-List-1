<p><p>The Lead Acid (SLA) Battery Market consists of two main types: AGM batteries and GEL batteries. AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) batteries use an electrolyte suspended in a fiberglass mat, making them spill-proof and maintenance-free. They are commonly used in vehicles, UPS systems, and solar applications. On the other hand, GEL batteries use a gelified electrolyte that prevents leakage and is resistant to vibration. They are best suited for deep-cycle applications like renewable energy systems and medical equipment. Both AGM and GEL batteries offer reliable power storage solutions with their own unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
