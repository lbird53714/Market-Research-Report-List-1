Scattered Type describes bamboo forests that are spread across multiple locations, requiring individual efforts in sourcing and processing. Mixed Type refers to a combination of both cluster and scattered bamboo forests, creating a diverse market with varying harvesting and processing methods. These different market types determine the availability, accessibility, and efficiency of bamboo raw materials for the industry.</p></p>
