<p><p>Soft Magnetic Composites (SMCs) are a type of advanced magnetic material used extensively in various industries. Soft ferrite SMCs offer high resistivity, low coercivity, and superior magnetic properties, making them suitable for applications in electronics and telecommunications. Electrical steel SMCs exhibit low magnetic loss and high permeability, making them ideal for electrical machinery and power transformers. Lastly, the "others" category includes SMCs with unique compositions and properties, catering to specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
