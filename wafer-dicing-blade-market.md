<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The wafer dicing blade market consists of two main types: hub dicing blades and hubless dicing blades. Hub dicing blades are equipped with a central hub that holds the blade in place during the dicing process. These blades offer better stability and accuracy but may be limited in terms of the maximum achievable cut depth. On the other hand, hubless dicing blades do not have a central hub, providing higher cut depths and enabling more efficient and precise cuts. However, they may be less stable compared to hub blades.</p></p>
