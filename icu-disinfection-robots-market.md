<p><p>ICU Disinfection Robots are utilized to maintain a clean and germ-free environment in hospital intensive care units (ICUs). Two types of disinfection technologies commonly used are UV-C and HPV. UV-C technology employs ultraviolet light to destroy viruses, bacteria, and other pathogens on surfaces and in the air. HPV (Hydrogen Peroxide Vapor) technology uses hydrogen peroxide to generate a vapor that penetrates the environment, killing microorganisms. Both UV-C and HPV play a crucial role in preventing the spread of infections and enhancing patient safety in ICUs.</p></p>
