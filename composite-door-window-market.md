<p><p>The composite door and window market includes two major types: fiberglass reinforced plastics (FRP) and wood plastic composites (WPC). FRP doors and windows are made by combining fiberglass and resin to create a strong and durable material. They are preferred for their resistance to corrosion, weathering, and high strength-to-weight ratio. On the other hand, WPC doors and windows are made from a blend of wood fibers and plastic polymers. They offer the natural appearance of wood along with the benefits of being waterproof, rot-proof, and low maintenance.</p></p>
