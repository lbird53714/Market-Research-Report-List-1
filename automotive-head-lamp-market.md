<p><p>The automotive headlamp market consists of two main types: halogen headlamps and xenon headlamps. Halogen headlamps are the most common and cost-effective option, providing a yellowish light and a low level of brightness. On the other hand, xenon headlamps, also known as high-intensity discharge (HID) lamps, offer a much brighter and whiter light, allowing for enhanced visibility during nighttime driving. Although xenon headlamps are more expensive, they are preferred by those seeking superior illumination and a more modern look for their vehicles.</p></p>
