<p><strong>The Multi-mode Microplate Readers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filter-based Readers</li><li>Monochromators-based Reader</li><li>Hybrid Readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-mode microplate readers are widely used in research laboratories for various applications. Filter-based readers operate by using specific filters to select certain wavelengths of light, allowing the detection of biomolecules that emit or absorb light at those wavelengths. Monochromators-based readers, on the other hand, use a diffraction grating to scan a range of wavelengths, providing flexibility in selecting specific wavelengths of light. Hybrid readers combine both filter-based and monochromators-based technologies, providing a wider range of applications and detection options for researchers.</p></p>
