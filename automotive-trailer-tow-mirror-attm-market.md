<p><p>The Automotive Trailer Tow Mirror (ATTM) market consists of two main types of mirrors: Rear-View Dimming Mirror and Side View Dimming Mirror. Rear-View Dimming Mirrors are designed to automatically dim the reflection from headlights of vehicles behind, reducing glare for better visibility. Side View Dimming Mirrors dim the reflection from headlights of vehicles approaching from the side, reducing the driver's blind spot. These mirrors enhance safety during towing by providing improved visibility and reducing the risk of accidents.</p></p>
